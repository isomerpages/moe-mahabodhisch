<h4>8World news reporter visits our school for a meaningful exchange and sharing session with students</h4>
<p></p>
<p>On 8 July 2025, reporters from 8world News visited our school for an interactive
session with students from the Young Reporter Programme and the Videography
and Journalism CCA.</p>
<p>They shared insights into newsroom operations and the daily work of a
journalist. Students participated actively and gained a deeper understanding
of the media industry. This meaningful experience not only broadened the
students’ perspectives but also sparked their interest in journalism and
news reporting.</p>
<h4>《8视界新闻网》记者走进校园 与学生进行交流与分享</h4>
<p>2025年7月8日（星期二），我校有幸邀请《8视界新闻网》的记者走进校园，与 “菩提小记者” 及摄影与新闻课外活动的学生，进行面对面的交流与分享。</p>
<p>活动中，记者不仅向学生介绍了新闻室的运作流程，还分享了记者的工作内容与日常经历。学生们踊跃参与，积极提问，进一步加深了他们对新闻媒体工作的认识与理解。</p>
<p>本次活动为学生提供了宝贵的学习机会，也激发了他们对新闻传播工作的兴趣。</p>
