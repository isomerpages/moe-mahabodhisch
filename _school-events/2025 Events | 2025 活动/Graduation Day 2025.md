<h4><strong>Graduation Day 2025</strong></h4>
<p>The 77th Graduation Day marked a significant occasion for our P6 students.
Commencing with an intimate ceremony in the classrooms, students received
their Graduation Certificates along with a Maha Bodhi Bear and cherished
their final moments as a class. Our VIPs also visited every class and shared
words of encouragement and inspiration with our students.</p>
<p>The focus then shifted to the main ceremony in the school hall, where
invited parents were gathered to celebrate the six years of growth together
with their graduating children. The ceremony featured a Symphonic Band
performance led and executed by the graduating Band members, and a gift
presentation of the students’ own handmade gifts and cards to the parents.
The day culminated in a heartwarming video montage capturing the growth
of the students over the years before the school song was sung.</p>
<p>We extend our heartfelt appreciation to our dedicated staff, the Parent
Support Group (PSG), alumni, and School Management Committee (SMC) for
their invaluable support and contributions, which ensured the success of
this ceremony. Singapore Buddhist Federation generously arranged for each
graduating student to receive a specially crafted Chinese name seal, further
enhancing the significance of the occasion. For the Class of 2025, this
celebratory event beautifully marked the final day of their primary school
journey.</p>
<h4><strong>2025 年毕业典礼</strong></h4>
<p>第 77 届毕业典礼是我校六年级学生学习旅程中的重要里程碑。典礼首先在各班教室内举行温馨的班级仪式，学生们领取了毕业证书及菩提小熊，并与同窗一同珍惜在母校的最后时光。贵宾们也走访了各个班级，为毕业生送上鼓励与祝福。</p>
<p>随后，典礼的焦点转移至学校礼堂，受邀家长齐聚一堂，与孩子共同庆祝六年来的成长与蜕变。典礼上，管乐团为观众带来精彩的演奏，表演由毕业的团员们倾力呈现。学生们也向家长献上亲手制作的礼物与心意卡，以表达感恩之情。活动尾声播放的成长回顾视频，记录了学生们多年来的成长历程。最后，典礼在全体齐唱校歌的歌声中完美落幕。</p>
