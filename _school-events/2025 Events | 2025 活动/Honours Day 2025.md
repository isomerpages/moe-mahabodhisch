<h4><strong>Honours Day 2025</strong></h4>
<p>Maha Bodhi School’s 77th Honours Day celebrated our students’ hard work,
resilience, and growth. The event affirmed their commitment to learning
and the values that guide them as self-directed, motivated learners.</p>
<p>A suite of awards was presented, including the Bodhian Star Award, Outstanding
Performance in Subject, Chinese Language Good Progress Award, Kwan Im Thong
Hood Cho Temple CCA Good Progress Award, Meetoh Foundation Award for Talent
Programme, Kuah Eng Scholarship Award, and the top 10% performance per
class. These awards recognised excellence, progress, and the drive to improve.</p>
<p>The celebration concluded with a warm tribute to our outgoing Vice-Principal,
Mdm Leng Bing Ling. The School Management Committee, School Leaders, Alumni
and PSG thanked Mdm Leng for her dedicated leadership, presenting her with
flowers and well-wishes as she prepares for her next posting. This was
followed by an appreciation of our Parent Support Group (PSG), with certificates
of appreciation presented in recognition of their steadfast support and
meaningful contributions to Maha Bodhi School.</p>
<p>Honours Day 2025 was a proud moment, marking our collective efforts and
achievements as a school community.</p>
<h4><strong>2025 年荣誉日</strong></h4>
<p>菩提学校第 77 届荣誉日庆典旨在表彰学生们的勤奋努力、坚毅不懈与成长。本次庆典肯定了学生们对学习的投入，以及他们作为自主、积极学习者所展现的核心价值观。</p>
<p>庆典上颁发了多个奖项，包括菩提之星奖、学科优异奖、华文进步奖、观音堂佛祖庙课外活动进步奖、弥陀基金人才计划奖、柯英奖学金，以及班级前 10%
优异表现奖。这些奖项旨在肯定学生们的卓越表现、显著进步，以及他们在自我提升方面的努力与坚持。</p>
<p>庆典尾声，大家向即将调任的副校长——梁冰凌女士致以衷心的感谢。学校管理委员会、校领导、校友会及<a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">家长志工团队</a>代表向梁副校长献上鲜花与祝福，感谢她多年来的悉心领导与贡献。随后，学校也向家长志工团队成员颁发感谢状，以表彰他们长期以来对学校的支持与贡献。</p>
<p>2025 年荣誉日，是全体师生的重要里程碑，见证了大家的共同努力与丰硕成果.</p>
