<h4><strong>Learn for Life Programme (LLP)</strong></h4>
<h4><strong>Learning through an Ethnic Arts Programme (LEAP)</strong></h4>
<p><strong>Our Distinctive Programme</strong>
</p>
<p>The Learning through an Ethnic Arts Programme (LEAP) is Maha Bodhi School’s
distinctive LLP, supported by PSE funding since 2006. Through rich learning
experiences in visual and performing arts, students develop aesthetic appreciation,
creativity, and cultural understanding.</p>
<p>LEAP also supports Character and Citizenship Education (CCE) and National
Education (NE) by fostering respect for diverse cultures and strengthening
social harmony in multicultural Singapore.</p>
<h4><strong>Programme Objectives</strong></h4>
<p>Through LEAP, students will:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Develop appreciation for the aesthetics of visual and performing arts</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Gain deeper understanding of Chinese culture and values</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build awareness and respect for diverse ethnic cultures in Singapore</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop creativity, confidence, and artistic expression</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Discover and nurture talents through progressive learning experiences</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Tiered Talent Development</strong>
</p>
<p>LEAP adopts a tiered approach to nurture students’ interests and talents:</p>
<p><em>Tier 1 (Mass Exposure)<br></em>All students experience a broad range
of ethnic arts through the curriculum and enrichment programmes.</p>
<p><em>Tier 2 (Peak Development)<br></em>Students with strong interest and
aptitude further develop their artistic potential through Co-Curricular
Activities, performances, exhibitions, and competitions</p>

<p>Individuals with strong cultural grounding</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Appreciative of diversity in multicultural Singapore</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Active contributors to the arts and community&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>The achievements of our students in performances, exhibitions, and competitions
reflect the strength and quality of the LEAP programme.</p>

<h4><strong>终身学习课程（LLP）</strong></h4>
<h4><strong>民族艺术学习（LEAP）</strong></h4>
<p><strong>学校特色课程</strong>
</p>
<p>“民族艺术学习” (LEAP）是菩提学校的特色终身学习课程（LLP），自2006年起获得学校卓越计划（PSE）资助与支持。通过丰富的视觉艺术与表演艺术学习体验，学生能够培养审美能力、发挥创造力，并加深对不同文化的理解。</p>
<p>LEAP 亦配合品格与公民教育（CCE）及国民教育（NE）的目标，培养学生尊重多元文化，促进族群之间的相互理解，强化新加坡多元种族社会的和谐精神。</p>
<hr>
<p><strong>课程目标</strong>
</p>
<p>通过 LEAP，学生将能够：</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>培养对视觉艺术与表演艺术的审美能力</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>加深对中华文化与价值观的认识</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>提升对新加坡多元民族文化的理解与尊重</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>培养创造力、自信心及艺术表现能力</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>在循序渐进的学习体验中发掘并发展艺术潜能</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>

<p><strong>分层培育艺术人才</strong>
</p>
<p>LEAP 采用分层培育模式，发展学生的兴趣与潜能：</p>
<p><em>第一层（普及体验）</em>
</p>
<p>所有学生通过课程与增益课程接触多种民族艺术，建立广泛的艺术体验。</p>
<p><em>第二层（深化发展）</em>
</p>
<p>对艺术具有浓厚兴趣与潜能的学生，可通过课外活动、演出、展览及比赛进一步提升艺术造诣。</p>
<hr>

</p>
<p>通过 LEAP，学生将成长为：</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>充满自信与创意的学习者</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>具备深厚文化素养的个体</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>能欣赏并尊重多元文化的公民</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>积极参与艺术与社会的贡献者</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>学生在各类演出、展览与比赛中取得的成果，充分展现了 LEAP 课程的优质成效与特色。</p>
