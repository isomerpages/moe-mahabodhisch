<p><strong><u>Character and Citizenship at the Heart of Learning</u></strong>
</p>
<p>At Maha Bodhi School, Character and Citizenship Education (CCE) lies at
the heart of our efforts to nurture students who uphold strong values,
care for others and contribute meaningfully to society. Anchored in the
school vision of <strong>“Inspired Learners of Exemplary Character,”</strong> the
CCE Department strives to nurture students of integrity who lead with purpose
and empathy while serving others with heart.</p>
<p>Aligned with the school mission of nurturing <strong>confident lifelong learners and compassionate contributors with a deep connection to Chinese culture</strong>,
our CCE programmes develop civic-minded and compassionate citizens grounded
in strong values and cultural identity.</p>
<p>Through purposeful learning experiences and authentic opportunities for
action, students internalise values, strengthen relationships and grow
into responsible individuals who contribute positively to society.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>Alignment to CCE 2021 Curriculum and 21st Century Competencies</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Guided by our department’s vision and mission, the school’s CCE programmes
are aligned with the <strong>CCE 2021 Curriculum</strong> and the development
of <strong>21st Century Competencies</strong>, equipping students with the
dispositions and skills needed to navigate an increasingly complex world.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Relationship between 21CC Framework and CCE 2021" src="/images/Relationship_between_21CC_Framework_and_CCE_2021_.jpg">
</div>
<p>Organised around three key ideas of <strong>Identity, Relationships and Choices</strong>,
our curriculum guides students in understanding themselves, building positive
relationships and making responsible decisions.</p>
<p>At the heart of CCE are the shared values of <strong>Respect, Responsibility, Resilience, Integrity, Care and Harmony</strong>,
which guide students to act with integrity, show care for others and contribute
positively to their communities.</p>
<p>Through explicit teaching, authentic learning experiences and everyday
teachable moments, students develop <strong>self-awareness</strong> and <strong>self-management</strong>,
strengthen their <strong>social awareness</strong> and <strong>relationship management</strong>,
and learn to practise <strong>responsible decision-making</strong>. Through
these experiences, they grow as confident persons, active contributors
and concerned citizens.</p>
<h4><strong><u>Focus Area 1: Character Building</u></strong></h4>
<p><strong>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <u>Positive Education through the HEARTS Framework</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Character education focuses on nurturing students’ values, well-being
and sense of purpose. Through intentional structures and meaningful learning
experiences, students develop the dispositions, social-emotional competencies
and resilience needed to thrive in school and life.</p>
<p>At Maha Bodhi School, Positive Education is enacted through the <strong>HEARTS Framework</strong>,
which shapes how students experience well-being, belonging and character
development in their daily school lives.</p>
<p>Guided by the <strong>PERMAH framework</strong>, HEARTS focuses on:&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Positive Health and Engagement</strong> – Creating safe and supportive
environments that promote students’ well-being.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Positive Action</strong> – Empowering students to take initiative
and contribute meaningfully through purposeful projects and service.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Positive Relationships</strong> – Building a caring school culture
where students support and uplift one another.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Positive Thinking</strong> – Cultivating a growth mindset, mindfulness
and resilience to navigate challenges with confidence.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>SAP Ethos</strong> – Deepening appreciation of Chinese culture
while strengthening understanding of Singapore’s multicultural identity.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Through HEARTS, Maha Bodhi School fosters a caring and empowering environment
where every student can flourish as a confident and compassionate learner.</p>
