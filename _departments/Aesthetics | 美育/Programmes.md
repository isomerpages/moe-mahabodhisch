<h2><strong>Programmes</strong></h2>
<h4><strong>The Spectrum</strong></h4>
<p>(Learning Beyond Classroom: MBS Bi-Annual Arts Exhibition)</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Spectrum showcased the diverse talents of our school community in
the MBS Art Gallery, celebrating students’ and staff’s artistic achievements
while promoting art appreciation. The exhibitions featured over one hundred
2D and 3D works, spanning traditional, contemporary, and digital art forms,
reflecting the vibrancy and diversity of visual arts.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The exhibition provided students with the opportunity to share their creativity,
thoughts, and emotions through art, inspiring pride and confidence in their
work. It also offered friends and families a chance to experience the joy
of art, fostering a love of creativity within our school community.</p>
<p></p>
<p>As the culmination of the Maha Bodhi School arts programme, The Spectrum
highlighted the value of artmaking and art appreciation in nurturing expressive,
confident, and creative individuals, while strengthening the school’s culture
of artistic exploration and self-discovery.</p>
