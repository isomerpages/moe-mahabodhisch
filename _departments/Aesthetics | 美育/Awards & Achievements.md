<h4><strong>Maha Bodhi School Shines at 2026 SYF Arts Presentations</strong></h4>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p>Maha Bodhi School is proud to celebrate our students’ commendable achievements
at the 2026 Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) Arts Presentations. Their dedication,
discipline, and passion for the arts have led to outstanding results across
our performing groups.</p>
<p>&nbsp;·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Chinese Orchestra – Accomplishment</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Guzheng Ensemble – Distinction</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Chinese Dance – Distinction</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Choir – Distinction</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Concert Band – Distinction</p>
<p>These achievements reflect the hard work and commitment of our students,
as well as the guidance and support of our teachers and instructors. We
would also like to express our heartfelt appreciation to our school leaders
and parents for their unwavering support and encouragement throughout this
journey.</p>
<p>We are proud of our students for striving for excellence and showcasing
their talents with confidence and pride. Congratulations to all involved
for bringing honour to the school!</p>
<h4><strong>菩提学校在2026年新加坡青年节艺术展演中绽放异彩</strong></h4>
<p>菩提学校谨此荣幸宣布，我校学生在 2026 年新加坡青年节（SYF）艺术展演中取得令人欣喜的优异成绩。同学们凭着坚持不懈的努力、自律的精神，以及对艺术的热爱，在各表演团队中取得了卓越成果。</p>
<p>&nbsp;本校成绩概览：</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 华乐团 —— 优等奖（Accomplishment）</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 古筝团 —— 特优奖（Distinction）</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 华族舞蹈团 —— 特优奖（Distinction）</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 合唱团 —— 特优奖（Distinction）</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 管乐团 —— 特优奖（Distinction）</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>这些佳绩充分展现了同学们的努力与坚持，也体现了老师及指导老师们悉心栽培与用心指导的成果。在此，我们也衷心感谢学校领导层及家长们一路以来坚定不移的支持与鼓励，陪伴学生走过这段成长旅程。</p>
<p>我们为同学们追求卓越、满怀自信地展现才华，并以优异表现为校争光，感到无比自豪。谨向所有参与并获奖的同学致以最诚挚的祝贺！</p>
