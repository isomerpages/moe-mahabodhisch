<h2><strong>Student Management Department</strong></h2>

values</p>
<h4><strong>Mission</strong></h4>
<p>To discipline with care with a commitment to support our pupils’ growth</p>
<h4><strong>Caring and Enabling Environment</strong></h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">

<p>At our school, we are committed to fostering a caring and enabling environment
where every Maha Bodhian is empowered to make responsible decisions aligned
with our school values. We believe that discipline is rooted in care, with
a strong commitment to supporting our pupils’ holistic growth.</p>
<h4><strong>Our Discipline Approach</strong></h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Student Management Department Picture 1" src="/images/Student_Management_Department__Picture_1.png">
</div>
<p>The school adopts a proactive and positive approach to discipline, anchored
in a strong foundation that nurtures both character and a sense of responsibility
in every student. This approach is guided by three key pillars.</p>
<p><strong>Preemptive Discipline</strong> focuses on establishing clear expectations,
routines, and structures to guide students’ behaviour and prevent issues
before they arise.</p>
<p><strong>Positive Discipline</strong> emphasises encouragement and guidance,
reinforcing appropriate behaviour through recognition, affirmation, and
consistent support.</p>
<p><strong>Restorative Discipline </strong>ensures that when mistakes occur,
students learn from their actions through reflection, accountability, and
making amends, rather than receiving consequences alone.</p>
<h4><strong>Student Leadership</strong></h4>
<p>Our school adopts <em>The Leadership Challenge</em> to develop student leadership.
In alignment with the Enhanced 21st Century Competencies, we aim to nurture
every student leader to be an inspiring role model. Each leadership practice
is intentionally linked to key competencies, supporting the holistic growth
of our students and empowering them to lead with confidence, character,
and purpose.</p>
<p>“Model the Way” emphasises Core Values, guiding students to demonstrate
positive attitudes and lead by example.</p>
<p>“Inspire a Shared Vision” develops Communication and Adaptive Thinking,
as students learn to articulate ideas and unite their peers towards common
goals.</p>
<p>“Challenge the Process” nurtures Adaptive and Inventive Thinking, encouraging
students to innovate and improve.</p>
<p>“Enable Others to Act” strengthens Communication skills, fostering teamwork
and trust.</p>
<p>“Encourage the Heart” builds Civic Literacy, shaping students to show
care, appreciation, and social responsibility.</p>
<p>Through this approach, every student leader is empowered to influence
others positively and grow into an inspiring role model within the school
community.</p>
