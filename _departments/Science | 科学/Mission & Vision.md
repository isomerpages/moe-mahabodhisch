<h1><strong>Mission &amp; Vision</strong></h1>
<h4><strong>Vision</strong></h4>
<p>Fostering curious minds with a passion for discovery and a deep appreciation
for the environment.</p>
<h4><strong>Mission</strong></h4>
<p>To cultivate a sense of curiosity and a passion for discovery in students,
fostering adaptive and inventive thinking through learning experiences.</p>
<h4><strong>Goals</strong></h4>
<p>We aim to equip students with a strong understanding of scientific concepts,
while nurturing their ability to think critically, solve problems, and
develop a deep appreciation for the environment.</p>
