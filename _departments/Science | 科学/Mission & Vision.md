<h2>Mission &amp; Vision</h2>
<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>Have a self-inquiring mind and love for the environment.</p>
<h3>Mission</h3>
<p>To nurture students to care for the environment, and develop them to be
creative, resourceful and independent thinkers with scientific skills for
life-long learning.</p>
<h3>Implementation of the Science Syllabus</h3>
<p>The approach towards the learning of Science is based on themes that students
can relate to in their everyday experiences, and to the commonly observed
phenomena in nature.&nbsp; The five themes are Diversity, Cycles, Systems,
Energy and Interactions.</p>
<p>The topics that are covered under these themes from P3 to P6 are as shown
below.</p>
